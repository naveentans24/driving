About Us

Welcome to Mentor Motoring School. Learning to drive is not just about getting a driving licence but is an essential skill for life. We love cars, teaching and road safety! Mentor is the only one motoring school that offers Theory & practical lessons in multi languages in Germany and is arguably the most progressive (how’s that for bragging?!). But seriously, the reason why our School has become so successful is because we deliver great value for our customers. We emphasise to all Instructors joining Mentor how important customer service is. We want our students to be completely satisfied and when things do occasionally go wrong, we strive to sort things out quickly.You can book lessons and manage appointments 24/7 online.

We are certainly not trying to be the cheapest Driving School! So being German we beleive in quality not quantity and “You get what you pay for” also we are determined to ensure all our customers receive value for money.

If lessons look so cheap that it looks ‘too good to be true’, there will be a reason why they are cheap. With Mentor, you are buying “value for money” lessons Ours Proper, structured training prepares you for the test in the most efficient way to pass your test at very first attempt and Mentor motoring school will give you safe-driving-skills-for-life.

Our mission is to teach you to safe driving skills.

The Mentor motoring schooloffers teaching method which is the best for motorists to complete their theory and practical testsin their very first attempts. Our teaching methodis the number one choice among drivers intending to be licensed in Germany and to drive or ride in the country. We help you achieve exactly that with our high-quality teaching method.

Mentor Learning System has transformed the disjointed array of learning programs into an integrated system chock-full of useful information on driving. Aimed at preparing you for the theory exam and promoting road safety measures, Mentormotoring school offers seamless access to all that is required to be a driver in Germany.
